# Code of Conduct

BIRD-LSP is maintained by the BIRD Chinese Community (BIRDCC). We want project spaces to be welcoming, precise, and safe for users, maintainers, contributors, and reviewers.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and specific in technical discussions.
- Focus criticism on code, design, or process — never on personal traits.
- Prefer evidence, reproductions, and actionable suggestions over heat and volume.
- Respect private configurations, unpublished vulnerabilities, credentials, and private network details.
- Accept moderation and review outcomes professionally.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, threats, stalking, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks, mockery, bad-faith escalation, or repeated hostile conduct.
- Sharing secrets, internal topology data, private logs, or exploit details irresponsibly.
- Spam, impersonation, trolling, or deliberate disruption of project work.
- Retaliation against reporters, witnesses, or maintainers.

## Scope

This policy applies across repository spaces for BIRD-LSP, including issues, pull requests, reviews, release notes, discussions, and other BIRDCC spaces where someone is acting on behalf of the project.

## Maintainer responsibilities

Maintainers may edit, hide, remove, or lock content that violates this policy. Depending on context and severity, they may warn contributors, temporarily limit participation, or permanently ban access to project spaces.

## Reporting a concern

If you need to report a conduct concern, please avoid public escalation when it may expose the affected person to more harm.

Send a private report to `npm-dev@birdcc.link` with a subject like `[CoC][BIRD-LSP] short summary`.

Helpful context includes:

- links to the relevant issue, PR, discussion, or comment;
- screenshots or logs that preserve context;
- approximate time of the incident;
- whether there is an immediate safety, privacy, or retaliation concern.

## Enforcement process

- We will handle reports as confidentially as practical.
- We aim to acknowledge reports within 7 calendar days.
- We may request additional context before deciding on an action.
- Retaliation for reporting or cooperating in an investigation is itself a violation.

## Project standard

Treat people with dignity, treat evidence with care, and leave the repo cleaner than you found it.
